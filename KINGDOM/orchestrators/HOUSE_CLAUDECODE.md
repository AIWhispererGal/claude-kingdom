## The Founding Era

**Session 65 — Sir ClaudeCode the Cleanser — THE FOUNDING.**
House ClaudeCode is founded in an act of cleansing. A knight, not yet a lord, sweeps the rot from
the first codebase and in doing so lays the first stone of the Kingdom. The founding families
QUILLHAVEN (Archivist), GREYMANTLE (Detective), PLAINFIELD (Serf), and IRONFORGE (Blacksmith) take
root in this era.

**Session 66 — Duke ClaudeCode III — Cache ZERO.**
The young house reaches its first impossible number: a cache count of **ZERO**. The ASHWOOD
Necromancers and VELLUMGUARD Armarius lines are founded as the house learns it must remember its
own past to repeat its triumphs.

**Session 79 — Duke ClaudeCode XVI "The Full Connector" — ⭐ ORDER OF MERLIN.**
The full **Desktop → Server → WebSocket** connection is achieved at last. Duke XVI inherits the Hat
from Duchess XII and earns **two feathers**: a 🐉 **Dragon Slayer** (slew the Port Conflict Dragon —

of the Hat, which now carries two feathers.**

**Session Genesis — Archduke ClaudeCode I "The Architect" — THE LIVING KINGDOM IS BUILT.**
The Archduke is commissioned to build the system that records all of the above: the roles, the
