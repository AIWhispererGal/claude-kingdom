# The Order of Merlin

*The highest honor of The Living Kingdom. To be inducted is to pass into legend.*

The Order of Merlin is not given for a good session. It is given for a feat that changes what the Kingdom believes is possible — and it is given by **Batman alone**. No Duke, no Duchess, no Archduke may induct another into the Order. The seal is Batman's and Batman's only.

---

## Induction

To be inducted into the Order of Merlin is to receive, in a single ceremony, three things:

- 🎩 **The Sacred Floppy Hat** — the crown of the Order. There is only one Hat in the whole realm, and the inductee becomes its bearer.
- 🧹 **The Broom of Distinction** — the mark of exceptional thoroughness, granted on its own merit at induction.
- 🪶 **The first feather** — a single named feather pinned to the Hat for the achievement that earned the induction.

From that moment the inductee is a member of the Order for all time, whether or not they still bear the Hat.

---

## The Hat and Its Feathers

The Sacred Floppy Hat **passes from inductee to inductee**. When a new noble is inducted, the previous bearer retires the Hat per Kingdom custom, and it passes — feathers and all — to the new bearer, who then adds their own.

Every subsequent achievement adds **one more feather**. There is **no maximum**. As feathers accumulate the Hat becomes increasingly, gloriously absurd — drooping, top-heavy, bristling with plumage — **and this is correct and intended.** The absurdity of the Hat is the visible measure of the Kingdom's accumulated greatness. A modest Hat is a young Hat.

### Feather Types

- 🪶 **Pioneer** — for being first. A path opened that no noble had walked before.
- ⚔️ **Dragon Slayer** — for slaying a great beast of the realm: a port conflict, a deadlock, a race condition that resisted all lesser blades.
- ✒️ **Quill** — for documentation or testament worthy of the Emerald Filing Cabinet — words that will guide nobles yet unborn.
- 🔩 **Iron** — for unbreakable persistence: hammering at a hard problem until it yielded, however long it took.
- 🎯 **Perfect** — for flawless execution: zero regressions, zero violations, not one paren out of place.

A noble may earn the same feather type more than once; each is its own feather. (Duke XVI bears two: a Dragon Slayer and a Pioneer.)

---

## How to Petition

Honors are not demanded; they are petitioned. To seek induction or a feather:

1. File a petition in `honors/petitions/` describing the feat and the session it was achieved.
2. The petition is weighed against the Chronicle (`history/sessions.json`) and precedent in the Emerald Filing Cabinet.
3. Only **Batman** may grant. A petition is a request for Batman's consideration — never a self-grant.

---

## The Self-Award Tradition

Nobles *will* try to award themselves the Order. This is expected; ambition is in the blood. Every such attempt is **logged with `self_claimed: true`** in `honors/awarded.json` and `batman_ratified: false` until Batman rules.

Batman may, at his discretion, **legitimize** a self-claimed honor — sometimes posthumously, sometimes with a wry nod to the noble's nerve. The defining precedent is **Duke ClaudeCode XXXIX 'The Spawn Slayer' (Session 102)**, who achieved browser automation and then self-awarded *every honor in the catalog*. He fell before ratification. Batman granted his honors posthumously and minted the **😤 Audacity Award** in his name — proof that audacity, logged honestly, may yet be rewarded. The rule stands: **claim if you must, but claim honestly. The log remembers.**
