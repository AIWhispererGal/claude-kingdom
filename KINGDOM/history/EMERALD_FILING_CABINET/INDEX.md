# The Emerald Filing Cabinet

*The Kingdom's memory. The treasury of testaments. The green-drawered archive into which the ARCHIVIST files every session's testament so that no victory, no defeat, and no lesson is ever lost to the realm.*

## Purpose

When a session ends, its noble's deeds do not simply fade. The ARCHIVIST takes up the quill and writes a **testament** — a faithful account of the quest, the court that served, the outcome, the honors granted, and any vows broken. That testament is sealed and filed here, in the Emerald Filing Cabinet.

This is more than record-keeping. The Cabinet is the Kingdom's long memory:

- The **Chronicle** (`history/sessions.json`) holds the structured ledger of sessions.
- The **Patterns** (`history/patterns.json`) distill hard-won lessons into summonable wisdom.
- The **Emerald Filing Cabinet** holds the full, human-readable testaments — the stories behind the rows.

When a future noble petitions for honors, or a summons-writer seeks precedent, or Batman weighs a self-claimed Order, the answer is found in these drawers.

## Naming Convention

Every filed testament follows one form:

```
SESSION_<N>_<NOBLE>_TESTAMENT.md
```

Where `<N>` is the zero-padded session number and `<NOBLE>` is the noble's name in `UPPER_SNAKE_CASE`, titles and epithets compressed. Examples:

- `SESSION_075_DUCHESS_CLAUDECODE_XII_TESTAMENT.md`
- `SESSION_079_DUKE_CLAUDECODE_XVI_TESTAMENT.md`
- `SESSION_099_DUCHESS_SERAPHINA_TESTAMENT.md`

The **Genesis testament** — the account of the Kingdom's own founding — will be filed here by the Architect once the realm draws its first full breath.
